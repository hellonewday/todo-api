Hướng dẫn sử dụng API này:

  1. GET: /lists
  2. GET: /lists/{commentId}
  3. GET: /comments
  4. GET: /comments/{commentId}
  5. GET: /comments/{todoId}

  6. POST: /comments/{todoId}
  7. POST: /lists

  8. PATCH: /comments/{commentId}
  9. PATCH: /lists/{todoId}

  10. DELETE: /comments/{commentId}
  11. DELETE: /lists/{itemId}
